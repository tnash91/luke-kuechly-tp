Luke Kuechly

The best defensive player of the Carolina Panthers

Luke Keuchly being a beast

  • Drafted number 9 in 2012 NFL Draft to the Panthers.
  • Played his whole career with the Panthers from 2012 to 2019.
  • Earned five Pro-Bowl elections.
  • Started in Super Bowl 50
  • 2012 AP Defensive Rookie of the Year.
  • 2013 AP and TSN Defensive Player of the Year.
  • Retired January 14, 2020.
